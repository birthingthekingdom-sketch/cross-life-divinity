UNDERSTANDING PROPHECY - COURSE INTRODUCTION VOICEOVER SCRIPT

[SLIDE 1: COURSE OVERVIEW]
Welcome to Understanding Prophecy. In this comprehensive course, you'll explore the office and gift of prophecy, and its vital role in the life of a believer. This course examines the spiritual gift of receiving and communicating messages from God, including divine insight, future events, and spiritual guidance within a Christian theological framework. Whether you're called to prophetic ministry or seeking to understand prophecy in your faith journey, this course provides biblical foundations and practical wisdom for discerning and operating in the prophetic gift.

[SLIDE 2: KEY TOPICS COVERED]
Throughout this ten-lesson course, you'll study essential topics including the biblical foundation of prophecy in both Old and New Testaments, the nature and characteristics of the prophetic gift, the difference between the office of prophet and the gift of prophecy, how to recognize true versus false prophecy, the role of prophecy in the church today, and practical guidelines for prophetic ministry. You'll also explore the relationship between prophecy and other spiritual gifts, and learn how to cultivate a lifestyle of listening to God's voice.

[SLIDE 3: LEARNING OUTCOMES]
By completing this course, you will be able to articulate a biblical theology of prophecy grounded in Scripture. You'll develop the ability to discern genuine prophetic words and distinguish them from human opinion or deception. You'll understand how to operate in the prophetic gift responsibly and with integrity. You'll gain practical skills for prophetic ministry in various church contexts, and you'll cultivate spiritual sensitivity to God's voice in your own life and ministry.

[SLIDE 4: WHO SHOULD TAKE THIS COURSE]
This course is ideal for pastors, ministry leaders, prophetic ministers, and believers seeking to deepen their understanding of prophecy. Whether you're exploring a calling to prophetic ministry or simply want to understand this important spiritual gift more fully, this course provides the theological depth and practical wisdom you need. It's perfect for those in leadership who need to evaluate prophetic words in their congregations, and for any believer wanting to grow in spiritual discernment.

[SLIDE 5: COURSE BENEFITS]
You'll gain a solid biblical foundation for understanding prophecy, develop spiritual discernment to recognize God's voice, learn practical guidelines for prophetic ministry, and grow in your ability to hear and respond to God's direction. This course equips you with the theological knowledge and practical skills to operate confidently in prophetic gifts, whether for personal spiritual growth or ministry leadership. You'll complete this course with a comprehensive understanding of prophecy and its role in the modern church.
