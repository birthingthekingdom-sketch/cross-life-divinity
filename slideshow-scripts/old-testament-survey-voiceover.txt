OLD TESTAMENT SURVEY - COURSE INTRODUCTION VOICEOVER SCRIPT

[SLIDE 1: COURSE OVERVIEW]
Welcome to Old Testament Survey. This comprehensive course provides an in-depth exploration of the Old Testament, examining its historical, literary, and theological dimensions. You'll journey through the Pentateuch, Historical Books, Wisdom Literature, and Prophetic writings, gaining critical insights into God's covenant relationship with Israel. Through careful analysis of key texts, cultural contexts, and theological themes, you'll develop a robust understanding of how the Old Testament lays the foundation for biblical faith and continues to speak to contemporary Christian life and ministry.

[SLIDE 2: KEY TOPICS COVERED]
This ten-lesson course covers the structure and composition of the Old Testament canon, the Pentateuch and the foundational covenants with Abraham, Moses, and David. You'll study the Historical Books including the conquest, monarchy, and exile narratives. The course explores Wisdom Literature such as Job, Psalms, and Proverbs, and examines the Major and Minor Prophets. You'll also learn about the historical and cultural contexts of ancient Israel, the development of biblical theology throughout the Old Testament, and how Old Testament themes and prophecies are fulfilled in the New Testament.

[SLIDE 3: LEARNING OUTCOMES]
By completing this course, you will be able to demonstrate comprehensive knowledge of Old Testament history, literature, and theology. You'll develop the ability to interpret Old Testament texts responsibly using historical-critical and theological methods. You'll understand the major theological themes that run throughout the Old Testament and their relevance to Christian faith today. You'll gain skills in connecting Old Testament prophecies and themes to their New Testament fulfillment, and you'll be equipped to teach Old Testament content with clarity, accuracy, and spiritual depth.

[SLIDE 4: WHO SHOULD TAKE THIS COURSE]
This course is essential for pastors, Bible teachers, seminary students, and ministry leaders who need a comprehensive understanding of the Old Testament. It's ideal for anyone seeking to deepen their biblical knowledge and improve their ability to teach Scripture. Whether you're preparing sermons, leading Bible studies, or simply wanting to understand the foundation of Christian faith, this course provides the scholarly rigor and spiritual insight you need. It's also perfect for those pursuing theological education or preparing for ministry leadership roles.

[SLIDE 5: COURSE BENEFITS]
You'll gain a comprehensive understanding of Old Testament history, literature, and theology grounded in scholarly research and biblical scholarship. You'll develop advanced skills in biblical interpretation and exegesis. This course equips you to teach the Old Testament with confidence and accuracy, to connect Old Testament themes to New Testament fulfillment, and to apply ancient biblical wisdom to contemporary ministry contexts. You'll complete this course with a deeper appreciation for God's redemptive history and the enduring relevance of Scripture.
