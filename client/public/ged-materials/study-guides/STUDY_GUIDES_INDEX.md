## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long should I study each day?**  
A: Study 1-2 hours per day for 8 weeks. Adjust based on your baseline score and target score.

**Q: Which subject should I study first?**  
A: Start with your weakest subject. Then study subjects in order of difficulty for you.

**Q: Should I study all subjects at once?**  
A: Mix all subjects throughout your study plan. This helps integrate knowledge and maintain interest.

**Q: How do I know if I'm ready for the test?**  
A: You're ready when you consistently score 160+ on full-length practice tests.

**Q: What if I'm struggling with a concept?**  
A: Use multiple resources (study guides, videos, tutors). Break the concept into smaller parts. Practice repeatedly.
