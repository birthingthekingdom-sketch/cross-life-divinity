Course Overview

Develop biblical leadership skills and character. This course develops your ability to lead with integrity, vision, and servant-heartedness in your ministry context.

Whether you're a pastor, church leader, or ministry director, this course will equip you with biblical principles and practical skills for effective Christian leadership.
