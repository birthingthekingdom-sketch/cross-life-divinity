Course Overview

Deepen your prayer life and develop an intercessory ministry. This course develops your ability to pray effectively and intercede for others with power and authority.

Whether you're a pastor, prayer warrior, or believer seeking to strengthen your prayer life, this course will equip you with biblical principles and practical skills for powerful prayer.
