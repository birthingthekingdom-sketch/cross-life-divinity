Course Overview

Learn to share the Gospel effectively and disciple new believers. This course develops your ability to evangelize personally and multiply disciples through intentional spiritual formation.

Whether you're a pastor, evangelist, or believer seeking to grow your ministry, this course will equip you with proven strategies for evangelism and discipleship.
