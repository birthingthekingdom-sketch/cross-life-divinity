Learning Outcomes

1

Synthesize Theological Knowledge

Integrate learning from across the M.Div. curriculum into a coherent, focused project that demonstrates comprehensive theological understanding.

2

Conduct Rigorous Research

Employ scholarly research methodology using primary and secondary sources to support original theological inquiry or ministry development.

3

Develop Professional Projects

Create a significant project that demonstrates professional competency in ministry or theological scholarship appropriate to your vocational calling.

4

Present Findings Professionally

Communicate your work in formats appropriate to your ministry context—whether thesis, article, curriculum, or community initiative.

5

Reflect on Vocational Calling

Engage in critical reflection on your theological formation and how your capstone project shapes your understanding of ministry and service.
