How Your Project Will Be Evaluated

🧠
Theological Depth
Your project demonstrates sophisticated theological understanding, engages primary sources, and contributes meaningfully to theological discourse.
📚
Research Quality
Your research is rigorous, well-documented, uses credible sources, and follows appropriate scholarly methodology and citation standards.
✍️
Professional Presentation
Your work is clearly written, well-organized, properly formatted, and presented in a manner appropriate to your field and audience.
🎓
M.Div. Integration
Your project integrates learning from across the M.Div. curriculum and demonstrates synthesis of core theological content and skills.
🌍
Practical Applicability
Your project is applicable to real-world ministry contexts and demonstrates how theological learning translates to vocational practice.
