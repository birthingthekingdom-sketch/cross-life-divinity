What is the Capstone Project?

The Capstone Project is the culminating experience in the Master of Divinity program. It represents the synthesis of your theological learning and demonstrates your scholarly competency through a substantial independent project that bridges academic study and vocational ministry.

🎓

Scholarly Integration

Synthesize knowledge from across the M.Div. curriculum into a focused project.

🤝

Faculty Mentorship

Work with experienced faculty advisors throughout your project development.

✨

Real-World Application

Apply theological education to actual ministry contexts or scholarly inquiry.

📖

Professional Competency

Demonstrate mastery and readiness for advanced ministry or academic work.
