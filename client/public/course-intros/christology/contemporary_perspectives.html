Contemporary Theological Movements

👩 Feminist Christology

Reimagines Christ through feminist perspectives, questioning patriarchal interpretations and exploring Christ's liberating message for women and marginalized communities.

Focus: Justice and Inclusion

⚡ Process Theology

Understands Christ within a dynamic, evolving cosmos where God works persuasively rather than coercively, emphasizing Christ's solidarity with creation's ongoing transformation.

Focus: Divine Persuasion

🌍 Religious Pluralism

Addresses how to understand Christ's uniqueness and significance in a religiously diverse world, exploring Christ's relationship to other faith traditions.

Focus: Interfaith Dialogue

🌏 Non-Western Christology

Develops contextual Christologies from African, Asian, Latin American, and Indigenous perspectives, allowing Christ to be encountered through diverse cultural and theological lenses.

Focus: Contextual Theology
