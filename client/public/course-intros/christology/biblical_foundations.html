Biblical Foundations of Christology

Old Testament Messianism

Messianic Prophecy

OT anticipates a coming Messiah who will bring salvation and restoration.

Royal & Priestly Themes

Davidic kingship and priestly mediation foreshadow Christ's role.

Suffering Servant

Isaiah's servant songs depict vicarious suffering for redemption.

Jesus' Self-Understanding

Messianic Identity

Jesus claimed to be the Messiah and fulfilled OT expectations.

Son of God

Jesus understood His unique relationship to God the Father.

Redemptive Mission

Jesus saw His death and resurrection as central to salvation.

Apostolic Proclamation

Resurrection & Exaltation

Apostles proclaimed Christ's resurrection as God's vindication.

Divine Lordship

Early church confessed Jesus as Lord with divine authority.

Cosmic Significance

Christ is the center of creation and redemption history.
