Understanding Christ

Christology is the systematic study of the person and work of Jesus Christ. It answers the fundamental Christian question: Who is Jesus, and what did He accomplish? This course explores how the church has understood Christ throughout history and what His incarnation means for faith and practice today.

📖

Biblical Foundation

Ground your understanding in Scripture's witness to Christ across both testaments.

⛪

Historical Development

Trace how the church developed and defended christological doctrine through the centuries.

🤔

Theological Depth

Engage major theological traditions and contemporary perspectives on Christ's person and work.

✨

Spiritual Significance

Understand how Christology shapes worship, ethics, and the church's mission in the world.
