Learning Outcomes

1

Articulate Biblical Foundations

Understand and explain how Scripture witnesses to Christ's person and work across both Old and New Testaments.

2

Trace Historical Development

Follow the development of christological doctrine from the early church councils through contemporary theology.

3

Analyze Theological Models

Evaluate major christological models and understand their theological implications for the church.

4

Engage Contemporary Challenges

Address modern challenges to classical Christology and evaluate alternative Christologies in light of tradition.

5

Apply to Christian Life

Integrate christological understanding into worship, ethics, preaching, and the church's mission.
