Christology in Pastoral Ministry

🙏
Worship & Liturgy
Christology shapes how we pray, sing, and celebrate the sacraments. Understanding Christ's person informs the church's worship and devotional life.
📢
Preaching & Teaching
Preachers must communicate Christ clearly and compellingly. Christological understanding deepens homiletical effectiveness and theological accuracy.
💬
Pastoral Counseling
Christ's incarnation and compassion model pastoral care. Understanding Christ's redemptive work informs how we counsel and support those in crisis.
⚖️
Christian Ethics
Christ's life and teachings provide the foundation for Christian ethics. Christology informs how we address justice, mercy, and moral discernment.
🌍
Mission & Witness
The church's mission is rooted in Christ's incarnation and resurrection. Christology motivates and shapes the church's witness and service in the world.
