Course Overview

Understand the principles and methods of biblical interpretation. This course develops your ability to properly exegete Scripture and apply hermeneutical tools for accurate biblical understanding.

Whether you're a pastor, teacher, or serious student of Scripture, this course will equip you with the skills to interpret the Bible accurately and communicate its meaning effectively.

What You'll Discover

Hermeneutical Foundations
Principles and history of interpretation
Grammatical Analysis
Understanding biblical language
Historical Context
Cultural and historical background
Exegetical Methods
Step-by-step interpretation process
