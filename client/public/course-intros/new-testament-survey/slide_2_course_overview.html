Course Overview

Experience the life, teachings, and redemptive work of Jesus Christ through the gospels, and witness the explosive growth of the early church in Acts and the epistles. This comprehensive survey reveals how the New Testament fulfills Old Testament prophecy and establishes the foundation of Christian faith.

Perfect for pastors, teachers, and serious Bible students seeking to deepen their understanding of Christ's message and the apostolic church.

What You'll Discover

The Gospels
Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John's accounts of Jesus
Acts & Early Church
The birth and growth of God's kingdom
Paul's Epistles
Doctrine, practice, and pastoral guidance
General Epistles & Revelation
Final apostolic writings and God's future kingdom
