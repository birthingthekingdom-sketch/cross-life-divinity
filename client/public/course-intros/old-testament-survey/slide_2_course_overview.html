Course Overview

Discover the rich history and theology of the Old Testament. This comprehensive survey traces God's covenant relationship with His people from creation through the prophetic books, revealing the foundational truths that prepare for Christ's coming.

Whether you're a pastor, teacher, or devoted student of Scripture, this course will deepen your understanding of God's redemptive plan and equip you to teach these foundational truths with confidence and clarity.

What You'll Discover

The Pentateuch
God's covenant with Abraham through Moses
Historical Books
Israel's journey to the promised land
Wisdom Literature
Proverbs, Job, and spiritual insight
Prophetic Books
God's messages through the prophets
