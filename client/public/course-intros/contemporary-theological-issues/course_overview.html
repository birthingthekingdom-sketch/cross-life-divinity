Theology in the Real World

The church exists in a rapidly changing world facing unprecedented challenges and opportunities. This seminar examines how classical Christian theology addresses contemporary issues while remaining open to new insights. We engage current theological discourse and develop informed Christian responses to modern challenges.

🌍

Prophetic Engagement

Develop theological critique of injustice and articulate prophetic witness to contemporary issues.

🤝

Dialogue Across Differences

Engage diverse theological perspectives and learn from those with different viewpoints and experiences.

💡

Constructive Theology

Move beyond critique to develop constructive theological responses grounded in tradition.

✊

Practical Action

Connect theological reflection to concrete Christian action and community transformation.
