How We Learn

📋
Case Studies
Analyze real-world scenarios requiring theological analysis. Apply theological frameworks to actual situations facing churches and communities.
🎤
Theological Debates
Engage in structured discussions of contested theological issues. Learn to articulate positions and listen respectfully to differing perspectives.
👥
Guest Speakers
Hear from practitioners and scholars engaged in contemporary ministry. Learn from those actively addressing theological issues in their communities.
📚
Student Research
Conduct individual and group research projects on contemporary issues. Develop skills in theological investigation and scholarly communication.
🤝
Interfaith Engagement
Dialogue with representatives from other faith traditions. Understand how other religions address similar theological and ethical questions.
