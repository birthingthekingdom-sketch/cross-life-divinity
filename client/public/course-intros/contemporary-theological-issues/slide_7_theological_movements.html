Key Theological Movements

✊ Liberation Theology

Emphasizes God's preferential option for the poor and oppressed. Theology must be engaged with liberation from economic, political, and social oppression.

Focus: Justice & Liberation

👩 Feminist Theology

Critiques patriarchal theology and reclaims women's experiences as sources of theological insight. Advocates for gender justice and inclusive language for God.

Focus: Gender Justice

🎤 Womanist Theology

Developed by Black women theologians, integrating analysis of race, gender, and class oppression. Emphasizes Black women's experiences and agency.

Focus: Intersectionality

🌈 Queer Theology

Challenges heteronormative assumptions in theology and advocates for LGBTQ+ inclusion. Explores how queer perspectives enrich theological understanding.

Focus: LGBTQ+ Inclusion

🌱 Creation Care Theology

Addresses ecological crisis through theological lens. Emphasizes humanity's responsibility for creation and God's care for all creation.

Focus: Environmental Justice
