Learning Outcomes

1

Identify Contemporary Issues

Recognize and analyze major theological issues facing the contemporary church with cultural awareness and theological insight.

2

Understand Diverse Perspectives

Comprehend multiple theological approaches to contemporary challenges, including liberation, feminist, and contextual theologies.

3

Develop Theological Responses

Formulate informed Christian responses to contemporary issues grounded in tradition and attentive to the present context.

4

Engage in Dialogue

Participate in constructive theological conversation across differences with respect, humility, and intellectual rigor.

5

Apply to Christian Practice

Connect theological reflection to personal and communal Christian practice, witness, and action in the world.
