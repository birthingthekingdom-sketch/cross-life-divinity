Major Topics We Explore

⚖️ Justice & Reconciliation

Liberation theology, racial justice, economic justice, and the church's role in addressing systemic oppression and promoting reconciliation.

🤝 Pluralism & Interfaith

Religious diversity, Christian exclusivism and inclusivism, interfaith cooperation, and Christ's relationship to other faith traditions.

👥 Gender & Sexuality

Feminist theology, LGBTQ+ inclusion, gender justice, embodiment, and the church's response to evolving understandings of identity.

🌱 Environmental Theology

Creation care, climate justice, ecological ethics, sustainability, and the church's responsibility for the created world.

💻 Technology & Digital Ethics

Artificial intelligence, social media, virtual community, digital justice, and the theological implications of technological change.

🏛️ Church & Society

Secularization, political theology, prophetic witness, Christian activism, and the church's engagement with public life.

🌏 Global Christianity

Majority World theology, postcolonial perspectives, indigenous Christianity, and contextual approaches from diverse cultures.
