Church Administration

Course Covers

  • Governance Structures
  • Financial Stewardship
  • Legal Compliance
  • Staff Management
  • Volunteer Mobilization
  • Facilities Management
  • Strategic Planning

This practical course equips ministry leaders with essential skills for effective church management and organizational leadership. Students will study governance structures, financial stewardship, legal compliance, staff management, volunteer mobilization, facilities management, and strategic planning. The curriculum balances theological foundations with administrative best practices, addressing the unique challenges of leading nonprofit religious organizations while maintaining accountability, sustainability, and kingdom-focused priorities.
