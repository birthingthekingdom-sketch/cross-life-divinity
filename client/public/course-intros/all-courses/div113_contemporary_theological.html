Contemporary Theological Issues

This course explores current theological challenges and developments in the modern church. Students engage with contemporary issues that shape Christian thought and practice, examining how the church responds to pluralistic culture, scientific advancement, and social concerns. The curriculum addresses emerging theological movements and equips students to think critically about faith in the contemporary context while maintaining biblical fidelity and theological integrity.

10 Lessons

Course Covers

  • Religious Pluralism
  • Science & Faith
  • Social Justice Theology
  • Emerging Movements
  • Cultural Engagement
  • Contemporary Debates
