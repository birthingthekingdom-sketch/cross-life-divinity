Christian Leadership

This practical course provides comprehensive training in Christian leadership principles and practices. Students explore servant leadership models, vision casting, team development, organizational management, and strategic decision-making. The curriculum integrates biblical foundations with contemporary leadership best practices, addressing ethical challenges, cultural sensitivity, and adaptive leadership in diverse ministry contexts.
