Deliverance Ministry

This comprehensive course explores the biblical foundations and practical applications of deliverance ministry. Students will study the nature of spiritual oppression, demonic influence, and the authority believers have in Christ to minister freedom and healing. The curriculum covers biblical case studies, theological frameworks, and practical protocols for conducting deliverance sessions with pastoral sensitivity and spiritual authority.

10 Lessons

Biblical Foundations

Understanding spiritual oppression and demonic influence through Scripture, examining biblical accounts and theological principles.

Authority in Christ

Exploring the believer's authority and power in Christ to minister freedom, healing, and spiritual liberation.

Practical Protocols

Learning effective methods and procedures for conducting deliverance sessions with pastoral care and spiritual authority.

Ongoing Support

Establishing systems for sustained freedom and spiritual growth following deliverance ministry.
