Pastoral Counseling

Content Areas

  • Pastoral Care Role
  • Grief & Loss
  • Trauma & Crisis
  • Marriage & Family
  • Addiction Issues
  • Mental Health
  • Spiritual Crises

This integrative course prepares students for the pastoral care and counseling dimensions of ministry by combining biblical wisdom, theological reflection, and counseling theory. Students explore the pastor's role as shepherd and counselor, addressing common issues such as grief, trauma, marriage and family conflicts, addiction, mental health, and spiritual crises. The curriculum emphasizes biblical counseling approaches while incorporating insights from psychology and therapeutic best practices.
