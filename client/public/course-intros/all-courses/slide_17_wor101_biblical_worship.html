Biblical Worship

This transformative course explores the biblical foundations and theology of Christian worship, equipping students to lead worship that honors God and transforms hearts. Students will examine worship throughout Scripture, study the theology of praise, explore various worship expressions and styles, and learn principles for creating authentic worship experiences.

10 Lessons

Theological Foundations

Biblical foundations and theology of praise, worship throughout Scripture, and the centrality of worship to Christian discipleship.

Worship Expressions

Various worship expressions and styles, contemporary worship challenges, and principles for creating authentic worship experiences.

Leadership & Impact

Leading worship that honors God and transforms hearts, musical excellence, spiritual authenticity, and community formation.
