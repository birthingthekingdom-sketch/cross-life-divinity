Discovering Spiritual Gifts

This empowering course provides a comprehensive exploration of spiritual gifts and their role in the life and ministry of the church. Students will examine biblical teachings on charismata, explore various gift categories, and learn to identify and develop their personal spiritual gifts. The course emphasizes that every believer is gifted for service and equips students to build gift-based ministry teams that maximize kingdom impact.
