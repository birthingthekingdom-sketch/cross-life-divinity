Christology

In-depth study of the person and work of Jesus Christ. This course examines biblical foundations, historical development of Christological doctrine, contemporary debates, and implications for Christian faith and practice. Students will explore the incarnation, redemptive work, resurrection, ascension, and the centrality of Christ to all Christian theology and discipleship.

10 Lessons

Course Covers

  • Biblical Foundations
  • Incarnation
  • Redemptive Work
  • Resurrection & Ascension
  • Historical Doctrine
  • Contemporary Debates
  • Practical Implications
