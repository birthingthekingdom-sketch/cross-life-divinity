Capstone Project

This culminating course requires students to integrate knowledge from previous courses into a comprehensive theological research project or ministry portfolio. Students demonstrate mastery of theological concepts, research methodology, and practical application by developing a substantial work that reflects their learning journey and prepares them for advanced ministry or academic pursuits.

8 Lessons

Project Components

  • Proposal Development
  • Research Methodology
  • Literature Review
  • Theological Analysis
  • Practical Application
  • Final Presentation
  • Peer Review
