## Implementation Instructions

### How to Add Videos to Your Platform:

1. **Copy the YouTube URL** from the recommendations above
2. **Log into Admin Dashboard**
3. **Navigate to Course Management**
4. **Select the specific course** (e.g., DIV101 - Introduction to Theology)
5. **Find the "Video URL" field** in the course settings
6. **Paste the YouTube URL**
7. **Save changes**
8. **Preview the course** to ensure video displays correctly

### Video Display Tips:

- Videos will automatically embed from YouTube/Vimeo links
- Make sure the video is set to "public" or "unlisted" (not private)
- Test on mobile devices to ensure responsive display
- Consider adding a brief text introduction above the video

## Quality Criteria Used for Selection

All recommended videos were selected based on:

✅ **Theological Soundness** - Orthodox Christian teaching  
✅ **Professional Quality** - Clear audio, good video production  
✅ **Engaging Content** - Holds viewer attention  
✅ **Appropriate Length** - 5-20 minutes ideal for introductions  
✅ **Accessibility** - Free and publicly available on YouTube  
✅ **Credible Teachers** - Seminary professors, respected pastors, established ministries  
✅ **Relevance** - Directly addresses the course topic

## Alternative: Create Your Own Introduction Videos

If you prefer to create custom introduction videos for a more branded experience:

### Benefits:
- Personalized welcome from CLSD leadership
- Explain CLSD's specific approach to the topic
- Build stronger connection with students
- Complete control over content and messaging

### Simple Setup:
- Use Zoom to record yourself introducing the course (5-10 minutes)
- Upload to YouTube as "unlisted" (not searchable, but accessible via link)
- Add the YouTube link to your course settings

### Script Template:
"Welcome to [Course Name] at Cross Life School of Divinity. I'm [Your Name], and I'm excited to guide you through this journey of [course topic]. In this course, you'll learn [key learning objectives]. Whether you're a [target audience], this course will equip you with [key benefits]. Let's get started!"
