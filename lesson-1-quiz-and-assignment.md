## Written Assignment Prompt

### Complete Assignment: Evangelism Foundations

**Total Word Count**: 1,200-1,850 words

---

### Part 1: Exegetical Paper (500-750 words)

Choose **ONE** of the following passages and write a detailed exegetical analysis:

- Matthew 28:18-20
- Romans 3:23
- Romans 6:23
- Romans 5:8

**Your paper must include:**

1. **Context**: Historical and literary context of the passage
2. **Greek Word Study**: Analyze at least 3 key Greek terms (use lexicons like BDAG or Thayer's)
3. **Theological Significance**: What does this passage teach about God, humanity, sin, or salvation?
4. **Application to Evangelism**: How does this passage inform or motivate evangelistic ministry?
5. **Citations**: Use Turabian or SBL format; minimum 3 scholarly sources (commentaries, theological dictionaries, or journal articles)

---

### Part 2: Personal Testimony (300-500 words)

Write your personal testimony following the three-part structure taught in this lesson:

1. **Before Christ**: Your life before salvation (2-3 sentences)
2. **Conversion**: How you came to faith in Christ (3-4 sentences)
3. **After Christ**: How Christ has transformed you (2-3 sentences)

**Evaluation Criteria**:
- Does it clearly present the gospel?
- Is it appropriate for sharing with unbelievers?
- Does it glorify Christ rather than sin or self?
- Is it authentic and relatable?

---

### Part 3: Evangelistic Strategy (400-600 words)

Develop a practical evangelism strategy for your specific ministry context. Address the following:

1. **Target Audience**: Who are you trying to reach? (age, culture, religious background, etc.)
2. **Barriers**: What obstacles (cultural, intellectual, emotional) prevent them from hearing or receiving the gospel?
3. **Method**: Which evangelistic approach(es) from Acts will you employ? (house-to-house, public proclamation, reasoning, marketplace dialogue)
4. **Gospel Presentation**: How will you clearly communicate sin, Christ's substitutionary death, and the call to faith?
5. **Follow-Up**: How will you disciple new believers?

---

### Submission Requirements

- **Format**: Double-spaced, 12-point Times New Roman font, 1-inch margins
- **Length**: 1,200-1,850 words total
- **Citations**: Minimum 5 scholarly sources (Bible commentaries, theological books, or journal articles)
- **Citation Style**: Turabian or SBL format
- **Due Date**: End of Week 1
- **Submission**: Upload as PDF or Word document through the course portal

---

### Grading Rubric

| Component | Weight | Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Exegetical Paper** | 40% | Accuracy of exegesis, depth of Greek word study, theological insight, proper citations |
| **Personal Testimony** | 30% | Gospel clarity, appropriateness, Christ-centeredness, authenticity |
| **Evangelistic Strategy** | 30% | Contextual awareness, biblical methodology, practical application, follow-up plan |

**Total**: 100 points
