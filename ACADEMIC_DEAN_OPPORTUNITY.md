# Academic Dean Opportunity
## Cross Life School of Divinity

---

## The Opportunity

Cross Life School of Divinity is seeking an **Academic Dean** to provide scholarly oversight and academic credibility as we equip ministry leaders worldwide through online theological education. This is a **voluntary position** (5-10 hours/month) ideal for someone who wants to invest in the next generation while maintaining their current commitments.

---

## About CLSD

We're an online theological education platform offering CLAC-accredited courses to ministry leaders across the United States. With 18 courses, 170 lessons, and growing enrollment, we're making quality theological training accessible and affordable for pastors, chaplains, and ministry workers who might not otherwise have access to seminary education.

**Mission:** Empower ministry leaders worldwide to deepen their biblical knowledge, strengthen their ministry skills, and transform their communities through the gospel of Jesus Christ.

---

## What We're Looking For

**Education:**
- Master's degree in Theology, Divinity, or Biblical Studies (required)
- Doctorate (Ph.D., Th.D., D.Min.) strongly preferred

**Experience:**
- 5+ years teaching in theological education (seminary, Bible college, etc.)
- Curriculum development or academic administration experience
- Active ministry involvement

**Character:**
- Servant-hearted with a passion for equipping ministry leaders
- Commitment to biblical orthodoxy and evangelical theology
- Comfortable working in online/virtual environments

---

## What You'll Do

**Core Responsibilities (5-10 hours/month):**
- Review curriculum for theological accuracy and academic rigor
- Co-sign student certificates alongside the President
- Advise on course development and academic standards
- Participate in quarterly leadership meetings (virtual)
- Lend your credentials and reputation to the institution

**Flexible Schedule:** Work on your own time—no fixed hours or office requirements.

---

- **Official Title:** Academic Dean (use on resume, CV, LinkedIn)
- **Recognition:** Featured on website with full bio and credentials
- **Platform:** Influence theological education for hundreds of students
- **Access:** All CLSD courses for personal enrichment
- **Impact:** Shape the next generation of ministry leaders
- **Flexibility:** Maintain your current position and commitments

**Future Opportunities:** As CLSD grows, we're open to discussing advisory compensation, stipends, or other arrangements that honor your contribution.
