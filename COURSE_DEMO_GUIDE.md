## DEMO TIPS:

### **Do:**
✅ **Speak slowly and clearly** - Give people time to see what you're showing  
✅ **Zoom in if needed** - Make text readable for everyone  
✅ **Pause for questions** - Ask "Can everyone see this?" or "Does this make sense?"  
✅ **Highlight key features** - Use your mouse to point to important elements  
✅ **Show enthusiasm** - Your excitement is contagious!  
✅ **Keep it simple** - Don't overwhelm with too many clicks or pages  

### **Don't:**
❌ Click too fast - People need time to process  
❌ Show errors or broken pages - Test everything beforehand  
❌ Get lost in navigation - Know exactly where you're going  
❌ Spend too long on one page - Keep it moving  
❌ Forget to share your screen - Double-check screen sharing is on  
❌ Show personal information - Log in ahead of time, don't show passwords

## BACKUP PLAN:

### **If Technical Issues Occur:**

**Problem: Screen sharing doesn't work**
- Have screenshots ready to show instead
- Describe the platform verbally
- Offer to send screenshots via email after the call

**Problem: Website is slow or down**
- Have a video recording of the platform ready
- Use screenshots as backup
- Apologize and offer to schedule individual demos

**Problem: You get lost in navigation**
- Return to homepage and start over
- Laugh it off: "Even I get lost sometimes—that's why we have good support!"
- Have the course URLs written down to navigate directly
