## Position Overview

**Position Title:** Academic Dean  
**Status:** Honorary Position (with future growth opportunities)

## About Cross Life School of Divinity

Cross Life School of Divinity (CLSD) is an online theological education platform offering CLAC-accredited courses to ministry leaders worldwide. We currently serve students across multiple states with 18 comprehensive courses covering biblical studies, theology, ministry skills, and specialized training including chaplaincy certification.

**Our Mission:** To provide high-quality, CLAC-accredited theological education that empowers ministry leaders worldwide to deepen their biblical knowledge, strengthen their ministry skills, and transform their communities through the gospel of Jesus Christ.

---

## Position Purpose

The Academic Dean serves as the chief academic officer, providing scholarly oversight, ensuring theological integrity, and lending academic credibility to our growing institution. This honorary position is ideal for a seasoned theologian or ministry educator who wants to invest in the next generation of ministry leaders while maintaining flexibility in their current commitments.

## Required Qualifications

### Education
- **Master's degree in Theology, Divinity, Biblical Studies, or related field** (required)
- **Doctorate (Ph.D., Th.D., D.Min., or equivalent)** strongly preferred
- Degree from a regionally accredited institution preferred

### Experience
- Minimum 5 years of teaching experience in theological education (seminary, Bible college, or equivalent)
- Proven track record in curriculum development or academic administration
- Active ministry involvement or pastoral experience preferred
- Experience with online education platforms is a plus

### Competencies
- Deep theological knowledge across multiple disciplines
- Strong commitment to biblical orthodoxy and evangelical theology
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Ability to evaluate curriculum and maintain academic standards
- Collaborative leadership style
- Tech-savvy with ability to work in virtual environments

## Key Responsibilities

### Academic Oversight (Primary)
- Review and approve course curriculum for theological accuracy and academic rigor
- Evaluate new course proposals and lesson content
- Ensure consistency in theological perspective across all courses
- Maintain CLAC accreditation standards and compliance

### Faculty Development
- Mentor and guide course instructors and content developers
- Provide feedback on teaching methods and content delivery
- Assist in recruiting qualified instructors as the institution grows

### Student Engagement
- Co-sign student certificates of completion alongside the President
- Serve as academic advisor for select advanced students (optional)
- Participate in student webinars or Q&A sessions (1-2 per semester)

### Strategic Planning
- Advise on future course development and program expansion
- Contribute to long-term academic vision and accreditation goals
- Participate in quarterly leadership meetings (virtual)

### Institutional Credibility
- Lend your name, credentials, and reputation to the institution
- Serve as a reference for institutional quality and academic integrity
- Represent CLSD in academic and ministry networks as appropriate

## Compensation Structure

### Current: Honorary Position
**Initial Phase (First 6-12 months):**
- **Title:** Academic Dean (with full use of title on resume, CV, LinkedIn)
- **Recognition:** Listed on website as Academic Dean with full bio and credentials
- **Certificate Signing Authority:** Co-sign all student certificates
- **Professional Development:** Access to all CLSD courses for personal enrichment
- **Compensation:** Honorary (volunteer) position
- **Expenses:** Any agreed-upon expenses reimbursed (travel, materials, etc.)

### Future Growth Options

As CLSD grows and revenue increases, we offer multiple pathways for compensation:

#### **Option 1: Small Stipend Model**
- **Compensation:** $500-$1,000 per month
- **Time Commitment:** Same as honorary (5-10 hours/month)
- **Timeline:** When enrollment reaches 100+ active students
- **Additional Benefits:** Professional liability insurance, conference attendance budget

#### **Option 2: Revenue Sharing Model**
- **Compensation:** 2-5% of net tuition revenue
- **Structure:** Quarterly payments based on actual revenue
- **Potential Earnings:** $500-$2,000+ per month (depending on enrollment)
- **Timeline:** When monthly revenue exceeds $10,000
- **Benefits:** Directly tied to institutional success and growth

#### **Option 3: Part-Time Advisor Model**
- **Compensation:** $2,000-$3,000 per month
- **Time Commitment:** 15-20 hours per month
- **Responsibilities:** Expanded to include:
  - Teaching 1-2 courses per semester
  - Direct student advising (5-10 students)
  - Active curriculum development
  - Recruitment of additional faculty
- **Timeline:** When enrollment reaches 200+ active students
- **Benefits:** Health stipend, retirement contribution, professional development budget

#### **Option 4: Hybrid Model**
- **Compensation:** Base stipend + revenue sharing
- **Example:** $500/month base + 2% of net revenue
- **Structure:** Provides stability with upside potential
- **Timeline:** Negotiable based on growth trajectory

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I maintain my current position while serving as Academic Dean?
**A:** Absolutely! This honorary position is designed for someone who has other commitments. The 5-10 hour monthly time commitment is intentionally flexible.

### Q: What if CLSD doesn't grow as expected?
**A:** There's no financial risk to you as this starts as an honorary position. If growth doesn't materialize, you've still made a meaningful contribution and gained valuable experience. However, our current trajectory shows strong potential.

### Q: How soon could compensation begin?
**A:** We're currently enrolling students and generating revenue. Depending on growth rate, we anticipate being able to offer Option 1 (small stipend) within 6-12 months, with other options following as enrollment increases.

### Q: What theological perspective does CLSD hold?
**A:** We are evangelical and orthodox in our theology, emphasizing biblical authority, the centrality of Christ, and the power of the Holy Spirit. We welcome candidates from various evangelical traditions (Baptist, Pentecostal, non-denominational, etc.) who can work within this framework.

### Q: Will I need to create course content?
**A:** Not initially. Our 18 courses already have complete content (170 lessons, 850+ quiz questions). Your primary role is oversight and review, not content creation. However, as we grow, there may be opportunities to develop new courses if you're interested.

### Q: What happens if I need to step down?
**A:** We understand life circumstances change. We ask for a 60-day notice if possible, to ensure smooth transition. There are no penalties or obligations beyond fulfilling your current commitments.

### Q: Can this lead to a full-time position?
**A:** Potentially, yes. If CLSD grows to 500+ students and generates sufficient revenue, we would be open to discussing a full-time Academic Dean position with competitive salary and benefits. However, we're being realistic about the timeline—this would likely be 2-3 years out.

### Q: What support will I receive?
**A:** You'll have direct access to the President/Founder, administrative support for scheduling and communication, and all necessary technology/platform access. We're building a team environment where you're supported, not isolated.
