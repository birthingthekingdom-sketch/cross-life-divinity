### Avatar Specifications
- **Professional Appearance**: Suit and tie or clergy collar (pastoral attire)

- **Lighting**: Professional, warm lighting
- **Camera**: Medium shot showing upper body and face
- **Gestures**: Natural hand gestures emphasizing key points
- **Expression**: Warm smile, confident demeanor, pastoral presence
