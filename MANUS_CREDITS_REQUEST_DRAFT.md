## PROJECT SCOPE & REQUIREMENTS

### Content Creation (Primary Need)

**Bridge Academy Curriculum:**
- **5 Subject Areas:** Mathematics, Reading & Language Arts, Science, Social Studies, Writing
- **Lessons per Subject:** 10 lessons each = **50 total lessons**
- **Content per Lesson:** 4,300-6,800 words (approximately 5,500 words average)
- **Total Content:** ~275,000 words

**Lesson Components Include:**
- Lesson introduction and learning objectives (300-500 words)
- Core instructional content with examples (2,000-3,000 words)
- Practice problems with worked solutions (500-1,000 words)
- Key concepts summary (300-500 words)
- Practice test questions (1,000-1,500 words)
- Study tips and test-taking strategies (200-300 words)

### Platform Features Needed

1. **Student Progress Dashboard**
   - Track completion percentage per subject
   - Display quiz scores and performance analytics
   - Show certificates earned
   - Learning timeline/roadmap

2. **Quiz & Assessment System**
   - Auto-grading for multiple choice and true/false questions
   - Manual grading interface for written answers
   - Instant feedback to students
   - Score tracking and history

3. **Certificate System**
   - Automatic certificate generation upon completion
   - Professional GED prep completion certificate
   - Verification QR codes
   - Downloadable PDF format

4. **Email Notifications**
   - Trial start confirmation
   - Trial ending soon reminder (day 6 of 7)
   - Enrollment confirmation
   - Progress milestone emails
   - Failed payment notifications

5. **Payment Integration**
   - $19/month subscription setup in Stripe
   - 7-day free trial system with auto-charge
   - Subscription management (pause, cancel, upgrade)
   - Payment failure handling and retry logic

6. **Content Protection**
   - Copy/paste prevention on lessons (already implemented)
   - Student name watermarking
   - Right-click context menu disabled
   - Keyboard shortcut blocking
