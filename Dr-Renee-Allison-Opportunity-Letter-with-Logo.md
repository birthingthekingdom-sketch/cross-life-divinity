**Position Title:** Webinar Instructor

**Assignment Basis:** At-will (Cross Life School retains the right to terminate at its discretion)

- **Frequency:** 1 to 3 webinars per month
- **Monthly Earning Potential:** $75 – $225 per month

**Payment Schedule:** Monthly via direct deposit or check (within 15 days of month-end)

## Responsibilities and Expectations

### As a Webinar Instructor, you will:

1. **Deliver Quality Instruction**
   - Present engaging, well-organized webinar content
   - Maintain professional demeanor and communication
   - Respond to student questions and foster discussion

2. **Meet Scheduling Commitments**
   - Arrive 15 minutes early for technical setup
   - Deliver webinars at scheduled times
   - Notify administration of any scheduling conflicts in advance

3. **Maintain Professional Standards**
   - Follow Cross Life School's Code of Professional Conduct
   - Respect student confidentiality and privacy
   - Comply with all institutional policies
   - Maintain appropriate student-instructor relationships

4. **Provide Course Materials**
   - Submit course outlines and materials as requested
   - Provide supplementary resources to enhance learning
   - Maintain organized, professional course documentation

5. **Assess Student Learning**
   - Administer quizzes and assessments as designed
   - Provide timely feedback on student work
   - Submit grades and progress reports as required

6. **Intellectual Property Rights**
   - All course materials, lectures, and content created during this assignment are the exclusive property of Cross Life School of Divinity
   - You grant Cross Life full rights to use, modify, and distribute all materials
   - You may not use or share materials outside of Cross Life without written permission

## Independent Contractor Status

You will be engaged as an **independent contractor**, not as an employee. This means:

- **No Employee Benefits:** You are not eligible for health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off, or other employee benefits
- **No Tax Withholding:** Cross Life School will not withhold taxes; you are responsible for all tax obligations
- **Self-Employment Taxes:** You are responsible for paying self-employment taxes
- **No Workers' Compensation:** You are not covered by workers' compensation insurance
- **1099 Reporting:** Cross Life School will issue a 1099 form for tax reporting purposes
