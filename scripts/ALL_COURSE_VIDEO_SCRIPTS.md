## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR ALL VIDEOS:

### **General Settings:**
- **Video Length:** 75-85 seconds
- **Speaking Pace:** Normal (not rushed, not slow)
- **Background Music:** Soft inspirational music (10-15% volume)
- **Logo Placement:** Top right or bottom right corner throughout
- **End Screen:** 5 seconds with CTA

### **Branding Elements:**
- CLSD logo (always visible)
- Course code (displayed at start)
- Website URL (displayed at end)
- Color scheme: Navy blue and gold (CLSD brand colors)

### **Avatar Selection Tips:**
- Choose diverse avatars across courses (variety of ages, genders, ethnicities)
- Match avatar to course tone (scholarly for theology, warm for prayer, professional for leadership)
- Use consistent avatar for related courses (e.g., same avatar for BIB101 and BIB102)
