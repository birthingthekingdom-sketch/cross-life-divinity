# DIV101: Introduction to Theology
## Course Introduction Video Script (60-90 seconds)

---

**SCRIPT:**

Welcome to Introduction to Theology at Cross Life School of Divinity.

Have you ever wondered what theology really is? Or why it matters for your everyday life and ministry?

Theology isn't just for scholars in ivory towers. It's the study of God—who He is, what He's done, and how we relate to Him. Every Christian is a theologian, whether they realize it or not. The question is: will you be a good one?

In this course, you'll discover the foundations of Christian theology. We'll explore essential questions like: How do we know God? What is the nature of Scripture? How does theology shape the way we live, lead, and minister?

Whether you're a pastor preparing sermons, a ministry leader equipping others, or a believer wanting to deepen your faith, this course will give you the tools to think clearly and biblically about God.

This is a CLAC-accredited course, which means you'll earn continuing professional development hours while building a solid theological foundation.

At Cross Life School of Divinity, we're committed to making seminary-quality education accessible and affordable. You'll study at your own pace, engage with rich content, and join a community of learners passionate about knowing God more deeply.

Ready to begin? Let's dive into the study of God together.

Welcome to Introduction to Theology.
