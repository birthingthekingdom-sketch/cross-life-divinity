Course Description

This comprehensive chaplaincy training program prepares students for professional chaplaincy in various settings including hospitals, military, prisons, and corporate environments. Includes CPE (Clinical Pastoral Education) equivalency.

What You'll Learn

  • Crisis intervention and CISM
  • Spiritual first aid and pastoral care
  • Ethical practice and confidentiality
  • Interfaith and cultural sensitivity
  • Trauma-informed care
