### Our Course Catalog

**DIV101 - Evangelism & Outreach** (10 CPD Hours)
Master the art of sharing the Gospel effectively in modern contexts. Learn biblical evangelism principles, outreach strategies, and how to equip others for soul-winning.

**DIV102 - Prayer & Intercession** (8 CPD Hours)
Develop a powerful prayer life and learn the principles of effective intercession. Discover different types of prayer and how to lead prayer ministries.

**DIV103 - Church History & Reformation** (12 CPD Hours)
Journey through 2,000 years of church history, from the early church to modern movements. Understand how historical events shape contemporary Christianity.

**DIV104 - Spiritual Gifts & Ministry** (10 CPD Hours)
Identify and activate your spiritual gifts. Learn how to operate in the gifts of the Spirit and build effective ministry teams.

**DIV105 - Christian Apologetics** (15 CPD Hours)
Defend your faith with confidence. Study logical arguments, historical evidence, and biblical foundations for Christian belief in a skeptical world.

**DIV106 - Discipleship & Mentoring** (10 CPD Hours)
Learn Jesus' model of discipleship and how to reproduce mature believers. Develop mentoring skills and create discipleship pathways in your ministry.

**DIV107 - Worship & the Presence of God** (8 CPD Hours)
Explore biblical worship principles and how to cultivate God's presence. Perfect for worship leaders, musicians, and anyone passionate about authentic worship.

**DIV108 - Deliverance Ministry** (12 CPD Hours)
Understand spiritual warfare, deliverance principles, and how to minister freedom to those bound by spiritual oppression.

**DIV109 - The Fivefold Ministry** (10 CPD Hours)
Study the apostolic, prophetic, evangelistic, pastoral, and teaching offices. Discover your calling and how these ministries work together.

**DIV110 - Prophecy & Prophetic Office** (10 CPD Hours)
Learn to hear God's voice, deliver prophetic words with accuracy, and operate in the prophetic office with biblical integrity.
