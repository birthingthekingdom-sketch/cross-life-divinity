## Step 4: Complete Assignments

### Finding Assignments

1. Go to your course from the dashboard
2. Click on a lesson
3. Scroll to the "Assignment" section at the bottom
4. You'll see:
   - Assignment prompt/instructions
   - Due date
   - Submission status
   - Downloadable templates and rubrics

### Submitting Assignments

1. **Read the assignment guidelines** - Click "View Guidelines" for:
   - Assignment templates
   - Theological writing rubric
   - Formatting requirements

2. **Prepare your assignment** (typically 500 words)

3. **Upload your file** - Drag and drop or click to browse

4. **Click "Submit Assignment"**

5. **Confirmation** - You'll see your submission status change to "Submitted"

### After Submission

- Your instructor will review and grade your assignment
- You'll receive an **email notification** when graded
- View your grade and feedback in the lesson page
- If revision is needed, you can resubmit (version tracking enabled)

## Tips for Success

### Time Management
- Check the assignment calendar regularly
- Start assignments early (don't wait until the due date)
- Set personal reminders for deadlines

### Quality Work
- Read the rubric before starting
- Use the provided templates
- Follow formatting guidelines
- Proofread before submitting

### Engagement
- Complete peer reviews thoughtfully
- Provide constructive feedback
- Learn from peer feedback you receive
- Ask questions if instructions are unclear

### Technical Tips
- Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Ensure stable internet connection for uploads
- Save your work frequently
- Keep copies of your submissions

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?
- Make sure you verified your email
- Try "Forgot Password" if needed
- Clear browser cookies and try again
- Use incognito/private mode to test

### Access Code Not Working?
- Make sure you're logged in first
- Go to the `/enroll` page specifically
- Enter the code exactly: `WELCOME2025`
- Contact your administrator if issues persist

### Can't Submit Assignment?
- Check file size (must be reasonable)
- Ensure you're logged in
- Try a different browser
- Refresh the page and try again

### Not Receiving Emails?
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify your email address in profile
- Contact administrator to check email settings
