## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Tone & Style
- Warm, professional, welcoming
- Conversational (not overly formal)
- Confident but approachable
- Emphasizes value and affordability

### Pacing
- Read at natural conversational pace
- Total runtime: 2-3 minutes
- Allow for pauses between major sections

### Visual Suggestions
- Open with welcoming shot of presenter or CLSD logo
- Show course examples during "What We Offer" section
- Display pricing and key benefits as voiceover continues
- End with contact information prominently displayed
- Include CLAC accreditation seal

### Voiceover Options
1. **Professional voiceover talent** - Polished, professional sound
2. **Founder/Director recording** - Personal, authentic touch
3. **AI voiceover** - Cost-effective, consistent quality

### Platform Integration
- Embed on landing page hero section
- Add play button overlay
- Include transcript below video
- Link to courses page from video CTA
