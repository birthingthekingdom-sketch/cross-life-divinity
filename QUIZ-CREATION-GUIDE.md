# Quiz Creation Guide for Cross Life School of Divinity

## Overview

Your platform has a **complete quiz system** already built. You just need to add quiz questions through the admin interface as you review each course.

## Why This Approach is Best

✅ **Quality Control** - You review content while adding quizzes
✅ **Immediate Launch** - Platform is ready NOW
✅ **Flexibility** - Adjust questions based on actual lesson content
✅ **Efficiency** - 10-15 minutes per course = ~3 hours total for all 17 courses

## Quiz Creation Workflow

### Step 1: Access Admin Panel
1. Log in as admin
2. Navigate to `/admin`
3. Select "Courses" or "Manage Quizzes"

### Step 2: Select a Course
1. Choose a course to add quizzes to
2. View the list of lessons

### Step 3: Add Quizzes for Each Lesson
For each lesson, create **5 high-quality questions**:

**Question Mix:**
- 3 Multiple Choice questions
- 1 True/False question
- 1 Short Answer question

### Question Quality Standards

#### ✅ GOOD Questions Test:
- **Theological understanding** (not just memorization)
- **Biblical knowledge** (Scripture references)
- **Practical application** (ministry contexts)
- **Critical thinking** (analysis and synthesis)

#### ❌ AVOID:
- Trivial facts
- Trick questions
- Ambiguous wording
- Questions with obvious answers

## Sample Quiz Questions

### Example 1: Multiple Choice (Theological Understanding)
**Question:** According to the biblical teaching in this lesson on "The Nature of Prophecy," which statement best describes the relationship between Old Testament and New Testament prophecy?

**Options:**
- A) Old Testament prophecy was superior and New Testament prophecy is unnecessary
- B) New Testament prophecy completely replaced Old Testament prophecy
- C) Both testaments reveal God's prophetic word with continuity and fulfillment in Christ
- D) Prophecy ceased after the apostolic age

**Correct Answer:** C

---

### Example 2: Multiple Choice (Biblical Knowledge)
**Question:** Which Scripture passage provides the primary foundation for understanding the fivefold ministry gifts?

**Options:**
- A) Matthew 28:18-20
- B) Ephesians 4:11-13
- C) 1 Corinthians 12:4-11
- D) Romans 12:6-8

**Correct Answer:** B

---

### Example 3: Multiple Choice (Practical Application)
**Question:** How should the biblical principles of deliverance ministry be applied in contemporary church contexts?

**Options:**
- A) Only by specially ordained deliverance ministers
- B) Through faithful biblical teaching, prayer, and pastoral care within the body of Christ
- C) Deliverance is not necessary for believers today
- D) Only through dramatic public confrontations

**Correct Answer:** B

---

### Example 4: True/False (Critical Thinking)
**Question:** True or False: Effective Christian leadership requires both theological understanding and practical wisdom, with neither aspect being sufficient alone.

**Options:**
- True
- False

**Correct Answer:** True

---

### Example 5: Short Answer (Synthesis)
**Question:** In 2-3 sentences, explain how the biblical principles of prayer and intercession integrate with the broader theological framework of Scripture and apply to your personal ministry context.

**Correct Answer (Sample):** 
"Biblical prayer is rooted in God's character and His covenant relationship with His people, revealed throughout Scripture from Genesis to Revelation. Intercessory prayer reflects Christ's ongoing ministry (Hebrews 7:25) and the Holy Spirit's work (Romans 8:26-27). Effective ministry requires a robust prayer life that combines theological understanding with practical dependence on God's power and guidance."
