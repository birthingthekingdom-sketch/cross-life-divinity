# Church Announcement Script

**Goal:** Get 2-3 enrollments from your church congregation

**When:** This Sunday during announcement time (or whenever your pastor allows)

**Time:** 2 minutes maximum

---

## Full Script (2 Minutes)

**[Step forward confidently, smile]**

Good morning, church family!

Many of you know that I've been teaching theological courses for over 30 years. Today, I want to share an exciting opportunity with you.

**[Pause]**

How many of you have ever felt called to minister outside the church walls? 

**[Look around, acknowledge hands]**

Maybe in hospitals... prisons... or even in the workplace?

That's called **chaplaincy ministry** - and it's one of the fastest-growing areas of ministry today.

**[Pause for effect]**

I just launched a **Chaplaincy Training & Certification Program** through Cross Life School of Divinity.

Here's what makes it special:

**[Count on fingers]**

**One** - It's affordable. $325 total. Payment plans available. That's a fraction of what seminary charges.

**Two** - It's flexible. Online, self-paced. Finish in 3-6 months.

**Three** - It's accredited. CLAC-accredited certification recognized by hospitals, prisons, and military.

**Four** - It opens doors. Hospital chaplains, prison chaplains, military chaplains, corporate chaplains - all need this certification.

**[Pause]**

Now, I'm not here to sell you anything. But if you've ever felt God tugging at your heart to minister in these settings, this might be your next step.

**[Hold up flyer]**

I have flyers in the back with all the details and a QR code you can scan.

Or you can go to: **crosslifeschoolofdivinity.org/chaplaincy-training**

**[Repeat slowly]**

Cross Life School of Divinity dot org slash chaplaincy training.

**[Final line]**

If you have questions, grab me after service. I'd love to talk with you.

Thank you, Pastor, for letting me share. God bless you all.

**[Step back, sit down]**

This program prepares you for all settings. We cover the requirements for each.

## Pro Tips

### 1. Get Pastor's Approval First

**Email your pastor:**

```
Subject: Request to Make Announcement Sunday

Pastor [Name],

I'd like to make a brief announcement (2 minutes) this Sunday about the Chaplaincy Training program I just launched.

Many in our congregation have expressed interest in hospital, prison, or corporate ministry - this program equips them for that.

Would that be okay? I can send you the script if you'd like to review it first.

Thank you!
[Your Name]
```

---

### 2. Practice Your Delivery

- **Read it out loud** 3 times before Sunday
- **Time yourself** - make sure it's under 2 minutes
- **Speak clearly and slowly** - people need to hear the website URL
- **Make eye contact** - don't just read from paper
- **Smile** - enthusiasm is contagious

---

### 3. Bring Extras

- **Extra flyers** - people will want to take them for friends
- **Extra pens** - for the sign-up sheet
- **Business cards** - easier to hand out than flyers

---

### 4. Follow Up is Key

**The announcement alone won't get enrollments.**

**The follow-up will.**

- Text everyone who signed up within 48 hours
- Offer to answer questions
- Make it easy to enroll

---

## What to Expect

**Realistic Goals:**

- **10-15 people** will take a flyer
- **5-8 people** will sign up for more info
- **2-3 people** will actually enroll

**That's normal!**

Not everyone who shows interest will enroll. That's okay.

You're planting seeds. Some will grow immediately. Others will grow later.

## Bonus: Offer to Teach a Sunday School Class

**If your church has Sunday School or adult education:**

**Pitch to your pastor:**

```
Pastor, I'd love to teach a one-time Sunday School class on "What is Chaplaincy Ministry?"

It would be informational - helping people understand if God is calling them to this type of ministry.

At the end, I'll mention the training program for anyone interested.

Would that be okay?
```

**Why this works:**
- Gives you 30-45 minutes instead of 2 minutes
- More time to build interest
- Positions you as the expert
- Likely to get more enrollments

## Sample Sunday School Class Outline

**Title:** "Is God Calling You to Chaplaincy Ministry?"

**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

**Outline:**

1. **What is a Chaplain?** (5 min)
   - Definition
   - Different types (hospital, prison, military, corporate)

2. **What Do Chaplains Do?** (10 min)
   - Crisis intervention
   - Pastoral care
   - Spiritual support
   - Real-life stories

3. **Who Can Become a Chaplain?** (5 min)
   - Qualifications
   - Ordination requirements (varies)
   - Heart for ministry

4. **How Do You Become a Chaplain?** (10 min)
   - Training requirements
   - Certification process
   - Background checks

5. **Q&A** (10 min)
   - Answer questions

6. **Next Steps** (5 min)
   - Mention your program
   - Hand out flyers
   - Collect contact info
