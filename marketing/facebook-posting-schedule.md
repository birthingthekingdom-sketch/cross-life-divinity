**Metrics to track:**
- Reach (how many people saw it)
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Clicks (how many clicked the link)
- Enrollments (how many actually enrolled)

**Double down on what works!**

If Facebook Lives get the most enrollments, do more of them.
If testimonials perform best, post more testimonials.

## Pro Tips

1. **Post consistently** - Daily posts for 2 weeks builds momentum

2. **Use images** - Posts with images get 2.3x more engagement
   - Use Canva (free) to create simple graphics
   - Or just post photos of yourself teaching

3. **Ask questions** - "Have you ever considered chaplaincy?" gets more engagement than statements

4. **Use hashtags** - 3-5 relevant hashtags per post

5. **Tag people** - If you know someone interested, tag them (with permission)

6. **Share to your story** - Repost your feed posts to your story for extra reach

7. **Pin important posts** - Pin your best-performing post to the top of your profile

8. **Boost high-performing posts** - If a post is doing really well organically, consider boosting it for $10-20 to reach more people
