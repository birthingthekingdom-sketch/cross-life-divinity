# Get Your First 50 Students - Shoestring Budget Plan

**Goal:** Enroll 50 students in 90 days with minimal budget (under $100)

**Key Principle:** Leverage relationships, time, and strategic positioning instead of money.

---

## Phase 1: Warm Network Activation (Week 1-2) - Target: 15 Students

### Your Existing Network is Gold
You mentioned "lots of people I've talked to" want chaplaincy training. **Start there.**

**Action Steps:**

### 1. Personal Text/Call Campaign (Days 1-3)
- **Make a list** of 30-50 pastors, ministers, deacons you know personally
- **Send personalized texts** (NOT mass messages):
  
  ```
  Hey [Name], remember when you mentioned wanting to get into chaplaincy? 
  I just launched our certification program - $325 total, payment plans available. 
  You were one of the first people I thought of. Can I send you the link?
  ```

- **Follow up with a call** to anyone who responds
- **Goal:** 10 enrollments from personal contacts

### 2. Email Your Past Students (Days 4-7)
- Email the 50+ students who completed courses in 2024
- Subject: "New Chaplaincy Certification - Exclusive Early Access"
- Offer: **$50 alumni discount** (makes it $275 total)
- **Goal:** 5 enrollments from alumni

### 3. Church Announcement (Sunday Week 2)
- Ask your pastor if you can make a 2-minute announcement
- Bring printed flyers (design below)
- Offer to answer questions after service
- **Goal:** 2-3 enrollments from your church

**Week 1-2 Target: 15 students | Cost: $0**

- **Thursday:** Student success story

Join and actively participate in:

**Strategy:**

- **Every Wednesday at 7pm** - "Ask Me Anything About Chaplaincy"
- Promote it Monday & Tuesday
- Answer questions live
- End with enrollment link
- **Goal:** 2-3 enrollments per session

### 3. Personal Profile Posts
- Post about the program **3x per week** on your personal profile
- Mix of: student testimonials, program benefits, enrollment deadlines
- **Tag** people who've expressed interest
- **Goal:** 3-5 enrollments

**Week 3-4 Target: 10 students | Cost: $0**

## Quick Win Tactics (Ongoing)

### 1. Founding Member Urgency
- **"First 50 students get Founding Member status"**
- Benefits: Lifetime access to all future course updates, exclusive alumni network
- Creates FOMO (fear of missing out)

### 2. Payment Plans Prominently Displayed
- Many people can't afford $325 upfront
- **Emphasize:** "Just $54/month for 6 months"
- Makes it feel affordable

### 3. Testimonial Collection
- After every 5 enrollments, ask for testimonials
- Post them immediately on social media
- Social proof sells

### 4. Weekly Enrollment Deadline
- **"Enroll by Friday to start Monday"**
- Creates urgency without being pushy
- People procrastinate - deadlines help
