Request a formal letter stating:
- They accept CLAC-accredited credits
- How many credits they'll accept (e.g., "up to 30 credits")
- Which programs the credits apply to (M.Div., M.A., etc.)

## Pro Tips

### 1. Start with the Most Transfer-Friendly
Liberty and Grand Canyon are your best bets. Get them first, then use them as leverage with others.

### 2. Emphasize CLAC Accreditation
CLAC is a recognized accrediting body. That's your credibility.

### 3. Position It as a Win-Win
You're not asking for a favor - you're offering them a pipeline of motivated students.

### 4. Be Patient
Institutions move slowly. It might take 2-3 months to get responses.

### 5. Follow Up Persistently (But Politely)
Don't give up after one email. Follow up every 2 weeks.

### 6. Get on the Phone
If you can, call instead of just emailing. Personal connection helps.

### 7. Use Your Chicago Location
Moody Bible Institute is in Chicago. Trinity is in Deerfield (suburb). Use your local connection!
