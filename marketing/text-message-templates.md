# Text Message Templates - Personal Outreach

**Goal:** Send personalized texts to 50 contacts over 3 days (20/20/10)

**Key Principles:**
- Keep it personal (use their name)
- Reference past conversations if possible
- Don't be pushy - offer value
- Make it easy to say yes

---

## Template 1: For People Who've Mentioned Interest

**Use when:** They've specifically told you they're interested in chaplaincy


**Why it works:** References their own words, makes them feel special

## Pro Tips

1. **Don't copy/paste blindly** - Personalize each message with their name and context

2. **Send during business hours** - 9am-8pm, avoid early morning or late night

3. **Space them out** - Don't send all 50 at once. Looks spammy.

4. **Track responses** - Keep a note of who said yes, no, maybe

5. **Follow up** - If they don't respond in 24 hours, send a gentle follow-up

6. **Be authentic** - You genuinely believe in this program. Let that show.

7. **Don't be discouraged by "no"** - You only need 15 yes's from 50 people. That's 30%. Very achievable.
