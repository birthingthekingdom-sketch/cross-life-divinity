## Email Best Practices

1. **Send from your personal email** - Not a "no-reply" address. People should be able to reply.

2. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am** - Best open rates

3. **Personalize the greeting** - Use their first name

4. **Keep it scannable** - Short paragraphs, bullet points, clear CTAs

5. **Mobile-friendly** - Most people read email on phones

6. **One clear CTA** - Don't give too many options. Just "Enroll Now"

7. **Reply to responses quickly** - If someone asks a question, answer within 24 hours

## Pro Tips

1. **Send from a real email address** - Not "noreply@" or "info@". Use your personal email so they can reply.

2. **BCC everyone** - Don't put all 50 email addresses in the "To" field. Use BCC to protect privacy.

3. **Test your links** - Make sure the enrollment link works before sending.

4. **Proofread** - Typos hurt credibility. Read it out loud before sending.

5. **Track who enrolls** - Keep a list so you can celebrate and thank them personally.

6. **Don't spam** - If someone says "not interested," respect that and remove them from the campaign.
