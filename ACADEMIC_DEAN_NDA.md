## 1. PURPOSE

The Dean has agreed to serve in a voluntary capacity as Academic Dean for CLSD. In this role, the Dean will have access to confidential and proprietary information belonging to CLSD, its students, and its operations. This Agreement establishes the terms under which such information will be protected.

---

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

**"Confidential Information"** means any and all information disclosed by CLSD to the Dean, whether orally, in writing, or in electronic form, including but not limited to:

- **Student Information:** Names, contact information, academic records, progress reports, personal information, enrollment data, and any other student-related data protected under FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) and other applicable privacy laws

- **Proprietary Content:** Course curriculum, lesson content, quiz questions, assignments, teaching materials, videos, and any other educational content developed by or for CLSD

- **Business Information:** Financial data, revenue figures, pricing strategies, payment plans, business plans, growth strategies, marketing plans, and operational procedures

- **Technical Information:** Platform access credentials, software systems, databases, website analytics, and technical infrastructure

- **Strategic Information:** Partnership discussions, accreditation strategies, expansion plans, and internal communications

---

## 3. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information does not include information that:

a) Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement by the Dean

b) Was rightfully in the Dean's possession prior to disclosure by CLSD

c) Is independently developed by the Dean without use of or reference to CLSD's Confidential Information

d) Is rightfully received by the Dean from a third party without breach of any confidentiality obligation

e) Is required to be disclosed by law, court order, or government regulation (provided the Dean gives CLSD prompt written notice of such requirement to allow CLSD to seek protective measures)

---

## 4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE DEAN

The Dean agrees to:

a) **Maintain Confidentiality:** Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent from CLSD

b) **Limited Use:** Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing duties as Academic Dean and for no other purpose

c) **Protect Information:** Take reasonable precautions to protect Confidential Information from unauthorized disclosure, using at least the same degree of care used to protect the Dean's own confidential information

d) **Secure Storage:** Store all Confidential Information securely, whether in physical or electronic form

e) **Limit Access:** Not share login credentials or provide access to CLSD systems to any unauthorized person

f) **Student Privacy:** Comply with all applicable student privacy laws, including FERPA, and treat all student information with the highest level of confidentiality

g) **Return Materials:** Upon termination of the Dean's service or upon request by CLSD, promptly return or destroy all materials containing Confidential Information, including copies, notes, and electronic files

---

## 5. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

The Dean may disclose Confidential Information to:

- Other authorized CLSD staff or leadership as necessary to perform duties as Academic Dean
- Professional advisors (attorneys, accountants) who are bound by confidentiality obligations, with prior notice to CLSD

---

## 6. NO OWNERSHIP TRANSFER

This Agreement does not transfer any ownership rights in Confidential Information to the Dean. All Confidential Information remains the sole property of CLSD.

---

## 7. DEAN'S INDEPENDENT WORK

This Agreement does not restrict the Dean from:

a) Teaching at other institutions or engaging in other theological education activities

b) Creating and publishing the Dean's own original works (articles, books, sermons, etc.) that do not incorporate CLSD's Confidential Information

c) Using general skills, knowledge, and experience gained through service as Academic Dean

d) Discussing general theological concepts, teaching methods, or educational philosophy that are not specific to CLSD's proprietary methods or content

---

## 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

a) **Duration:** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues during the Dean's service as Academic Dean and for **three (3) years** following the termination of such service

b) **Survival:** The confidentiality obligations under this Agreement survive the termination of the Dean's service with CLSD

c) **Termination of Service:** Either party may terminate the Dean's service relationship at any time with 60 days' written notice. Termination of service does not terminate the confidentiality obligations under this Agreement

---

## 9. NO COMPENSATION WAIVER

The Dean acknowledges that this is a voluntary position. This Agreement does not create any obligation for CLSD to provide compensation, benefits, or employment. Any future discussions regarding compensation or advisory fees are separate from this Agreement.
