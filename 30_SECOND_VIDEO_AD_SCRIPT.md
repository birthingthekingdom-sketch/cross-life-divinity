## Production Notes

### Visual Assets Needed
1. **Hero promotional image** - African American male student in library (already created: `promo-hero.png`)
2. **Pricing promotional image** - South Asian female student with laptop (already created: `promo-pricing.png`)
3. **Platform interface screenshots** - Course dashboard, lesson page, certificate
4. **Cross Life School of Divinity logo** - High resolution, transparent background
5. **CPD accreditation badge** - Official seal or logo
6. **Text overlays** - Animated typography for key messages

### Audio Elements
- **Voiceover:** Professional voice talent (warm, authoritative, trustworthy)
- **Background music:** Uplifting instrumental, subtle orchestral or piano
- **Sound effects (optional):** Soft page turns, gentle keyboard typing, subtle whoosh for transitions

### Color Palette (from brand)
- **Navy blue** (#0A2540 or similar) - Primary brand color
- **Gold/Amber** (#D4AF37 or similar) - Accent color for text overlays
- **White** - Clean text and highlights
- **Warm tones** - Matching the library/study environment

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, serif or strong sans-serif (e.g., Playfair Display, Montserrat Bold)
- **Body text:** Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans, Lato)
- **Price callout:** Extra bold, large size for impact

### Animation Style
- **Smooth fades** between scenes (0.5-1 second transitions)
- **Text animations:** Fade in with slight scale or slide
- **Logo animation:** Elegant reveal at end
- **Minimal motion graphics** - Keep focus on people and message

## Performance Metrics to Track

### Social Media Ads:
- **View rate:** % who watch to completion
- **Click-through rate (CTR):** % who click to website
- **Cost per click (CPC):** Ad spend ÷ clicks
- **Conversion rate:** % who enroll after clicking

### Website Embed:
- **Play rate:** % of visitors who start video
- **Completion rate:** % who watch entire video
- **Engagement time:** Average watch duration
- **Bounce rate:** % who leave after video

### Target Benchmarks:
- **View completion rate:** 50%+ (excellent for 30-second ad)
- **Click-through rate:** 2-5% (social media ads)
- **Conversion rate:** 5-10% (visitors to enrollments)
