## CREDIT CONSUMPTION FACTORS

### Content Factors (Variable)
- **Research depth:** Simple overview vs. comprehensive analysis
- **Accuracy requirements:** General knowledge vs. test-specific precision
- **Examples/problems:** Few vs. many practice problems
- **Revisions:** First draft vs. multiple refinement cycles

### Feature Factors (Variable)
- **Dashboard complexity:** Basic tracking vs. advanced analytics
- **Grading system:** Auto-grading only vs. manual + auto
- **Integrations:** Stripe, email, content protection, etc.
- **Testing:** Basic QA vs. comprehensive test coverage

### Infrastructure Factors (Variable)
- **LLM tokens:** How many AI tokens for content generation/refinement
- **VM usage:** Compute resources for testing and deployment
- **API calls:** Third-party services (Stripe, email, etc.)
- **Data processing:** Database operations, file handling, etc.
